Welcome to OpenShift.io! To get started you will need to create a username, provide a contact email and connect your GitHub and OpenShift Online accounts. Your profile

Your username will be shown on your profile. You cannot change your username at this time. Think carefully, you cannot change your username. Username must be less than 63 characters, and can't begin with a dash or an underscore. They may contain lowercase letters, numbers, dashes or underscores.

Your name will be shown on your profile.

Your email will not be shown on your profile.

Connected accounts

You must connect your GitHub and Openshift Online accounts to OpenShift.io. This allows us to import and manage your source code, your pipelines and your environments.
